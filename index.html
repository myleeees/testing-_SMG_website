What We Do

Our Services

Integrated facade solutions — design, engineering, fabrication, and installation — delivered from one specialist team.

01

Curtain Wall Systems

High-performance unitised and stick-built curtain wall systems engineered for Australia's demanding climate, with superior thermal and acoustic performance.

02

Aluminium Cladding

Custom aluminium composite and solid panel cladding systems, fabricated in-house to exact specification and installed by our certified teams.

03

Structural Glazing

Advanced structural silicone glazing and point-fixed glass systems for striking architectural expressions with uncompromising structural integrity.

04

Windows & Louvres

Thermally broken commercial window systems, fixed and operable louvre blades, and integrated ventilation solutions for modern commercial buildings.

05

Facade Engineering

In-house engineering and peer review — wind load analysis, water penetration testing, and performance verification per ASTM E330/E331.

06

Maintenance & Remediation

Comprehensive warranty programs, scheduled inspections, emergency response, and facade remediation for commercial and residential buildings.
